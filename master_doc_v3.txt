0) Key Definitions

Roles

Customer: Uses Wiom Customer App + on-site actions.

Installer: The person performing on-site setup at customer home.

Typical: Technician using Expert App

Rare: Partner himself using Partner Plus App

Partner (Manager role): Uses Partner Plus App to manage assignment; can also act as Installer (rare).



Surfaces

Customer App (Wiom): booking + chat + happy code + rating + post-setup actions.

Installer App: Expert App / Partner Plus App (setup journey is mostly identical).

Wiom Systems: booking service, assignment, payments, notifications (FCM), Firestore listeners, router commands, device provisioning, events/states in backend stores.



Terminology Rule

All user-facing strings use "Net Box" (not "Device").

"Installation" label is updated to "Setup / सेटअप" in key places.



1) Partner Plus vs Expert App — Differences

Same (mostly identical)

Net Box setup journey screens, order, validations, gating, provisioning, happy code, rating prompts.



Navigation Difference (corrected)

⚠ CORRECTION (26 Jan update): Partner Plus App has "My Work" with To-Do / In-Progress tabs. Expert App has an additional "Tickets (टिकट)" tab (structure otherwise similar).



Other Differences

1. Partner Plus App has assignment control:

Partner can select "who will do the setup" at acceptance time.

Partner Plus App also has control to change the assigned team member from the Installation Detail page.

UI text/screens for reassignment are not fully specified in current artifacts; only the capability is confirmed.

Module 9 — Payment Collection & Verification (₹300 security fee)

Journey step: 8 of 15  |  Actors: Customer + Installer + Wiom payment systems



9.1 Payment Logic

Online payment is the primary path in PayG model.

Cash and online both converge to a single "Payment done" state; Autopay removed.



9.2 Screens & States

Payment pending (online): Installer prompts customer to pay via customer app (QR supported)

Payment pending (cash): "Collect ₹300 cash and confirm payment"

Payment checking: "Payment status is being checked" loader

Payment done: "Payment done" chip/tag

Customer payment incomplete warning: explicit messaging reinforcing ₹300-only



9.3 Backend Sequence

Online path: customer chooses online → Firestore notifies partner app → Create transaction API → Juspay → CAPTURE_PAYMENT event

Cash path: customer chooses cash → Firestore notifies partner app → cash payment button enabled → wallet/pay APIs

Both converge: Update booking in Dynamo → FCM notify apps → Unified "Payment done" state



9.4 Payment Gate (post-Aadhaar)

If payment still pending after Aadhaar (Module 7): system blocks progress until payment is confirmed.
